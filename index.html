Dance is an important aspect of every civilization, a cultural treasure that embodies the experiences of a community and the stories they lived out.

Ba-Ingles

This is a delightful dance from Cabugao, Ilocos Sur. Ba-Ingles is derived from the words “baile” and “Ingles” meaning English dance.

Difficulty: Easy

Polka sa Nayon

This ballroom polka was popular in the province of Batangas in the Spanish time. It was usually danced during town fiestas and in big social affairs.

Difficulty: Average
