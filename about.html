Philippine Dances

Philippine dances are more than just ancestral performances, they tell stories about our country’s people and the influence of many cultures we’ve come across.
These are 2 important terms to understand the dances better:

Figures

Figures are the different segments of the dance which are performed from Introduction to Figure 1 and so on until the Saludo.

Most dances are comprised of a number of figures which must be followed in order for completion.

Measures

Measures are the counts for each dance. Each measure will contain a certain amount of counts for the step, depending on the dance.

Steps have measures, and the counts for these steps add up to create a Figure.

Why Philippine Dance?

Because Philippine Dance is a cultural treasure that gives us an abundance of knowledge that bolsters our national identity.
